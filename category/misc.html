Despite my terrible record with this sort of thing, I keep hearing that it's good idea to maintain some sort of "web presence," so, uh... here we are, I guess.

Not a lot of plans to speak of for this space, to be honest. I've quarantined most of my rambling to Twitter, so if it's corny jokes, political commentary, or informal tech discussion you're looking for, best to head that direction. Otherwise, maybe I'll post some more nuanced thoughts or a Jupyter notebook here from time to time.

Perhaps the only thing you can count on is that this is where I'll be hosting the "master copy" of my résumé. After all, the goal here is to come out looking at least somewhat professional.
